Brownies Cockaigne from Joy of Cooking

Perfect when you want a warm chocolatey comfort food (so anytime!)

I will admit I have an above average appreciation for chocolate. A rich chocalate treat suits my mood most of the time, so this won't be the only chocolate recipe you find on this site! Brownies are a classic and this recipe from Joy of Cooking will not disappoint!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz unsweetened chocolate
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • optional: 1 cup chopped pecans
  • optional garnish: whipped cream, ice cream

Directions

  1. Choose your pan:
    • 9x13 for chewy brownies
    • 9x9 for cakey brownies
  2. Preheat oven to 350 F
  3. Grease pan
  4. Melt butter & chocolate in small saucepan, cool
  5. Beat eggs & salt until light in colour, foamy texture
  6. Gradually add sugar & vanilla, continue beating until thick
  7. With a few swift strokes stir in cooled chocolate until just combined. use a wooden spoon at this step
  8. Stir in flour until just combined
  9. Stir in pecans if using
  10. Scrape batter into pan, bake 25 minutes
  11. Cool completely in pan
  12. Cut to desired size, serve with garnish of choice
