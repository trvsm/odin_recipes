Classic Hamburger with a Side of Fries

A North American classic, this dish captures the feel of summer!

Though eating red meat is not the best ethical choice, a juicy hamburger is a special treat. Served with a side of french fries, a homemade burger is sure to satisfy!

Ingredients

Hamburgers

  • 1 1/4 lb ground beef
  • sprinkle of salt & pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • toppings as desired:
    • mayo
    • mustard
    • sliced tomato
    • lettuce
    • pickles
    • ketchup
    • relish
    • anything else you can think of

French Fries

  • 4 large baking potatoes
  • deep fryer or deep heavy pot
  • 3" peanut or other vegetable oil

Directions

Hamburgers

  1. Divide meat into 4 equal portions
  2. Form each into a patty 1" thick
  3. Sprinkle with salt & pepper
  4. Cook, on medium-high in skillet or on grill
  5. Cook 5 minutes per side, flipping once
  6. Place burgers in buns
  7. Add desired toppings and serve

French Fries

  1. Cut potatoes into strips 2"x1/2"x1/2"
  2. Soak in cold water to cover for 30 minutes
  3. Heat oil in fryer or pot to 350 F
  4. Drain and dry potatoes on paper towel
  5. Drop potatoes in 1 cup batches into fryer
  6. Fry until sputtering ceases (about 2 minutes)
  7. Skim potatoes with slotted spoon, drain on paper towel
  8. Cool potatoes at least 5 minutes
  9. Heat oil to 365 F for second fry
  10. Fry until golden brown, 2-3 minutes
  11. Drain on paper towel. Do not cover
  12. Lightly season with salt and serve!
